Saman Farid

Founder & CEO, Formic

▸ Robotics to make American factories more productive

I build robotics that increase the productivity and capacity of factories across the US. Before Formic, I ran the global investment fund for Baidu Ventures, focused on AI, machine learning, and how they reshape the way we work and live.

I'm also the founder of Comet Labs, an AI-focused investment fund and incubator. Earlier, I built and sold two technology companies — an e-commerce business and an IPTV infrastructure company — worked on alternative-energy infrastructure, and had short stints at Verizon, Microsoft, and Honeywell.

Outside of work, I care about youth empowerment and volunteer with East Bay Lights when I can.
